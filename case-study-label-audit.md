# Case Study Label Audit

Proposed labels for review. Conservative by design.

Legend:
- Industry = what kind of business this is
- Challenge = the core problem the work solved
- Channel = media/channel clearly central to the case study
- Region = market region supported by the case
- Outcome = what the case proves/delivers

---

## 1. Rakuten, TikTok Creative Optimization (2023)
- Industry: retail, e-commerce
- Challenge: creative performance, user acquisition
- Channel: paid social, tiktok, influencers
- Region: americas
- Outcome: cost efficiency, creative effectiveness, test & learn

Why:
- Clearly TikTok acquisition and creative testing focused
- Influencer/creator layer is explicitly part of execution
- Retail/e-commerce is clear, marketplace/shopping felt too broad
